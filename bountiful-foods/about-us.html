About Us

Founded in 1950, our family-owned fruit company has been providing the freshest and highest quality fruits to our community for over 70 years. Located in the heart of sunny San Diego, our orchards are surrounded by the beautiful mountains and fertile soils that make our region known for its delicious fruits.

We take pride in our dedication to sustainable farming practices and commitment to providing our customers with the best possible fruit. From our juicy peaches and plump strawberries, to our crisp apples and tangy citrus, we have a variety of fruits to satisfy any palate.
