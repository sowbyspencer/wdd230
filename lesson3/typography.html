Heading fonts are: 'Roboto Mono', Courier, monospace

The same width per character gives headings a 'blockier' feel good for headings. Monospace is also asociated with 'offical' documents lending to authourity.

All other fonts are: 'Roboto', sans-serif;

The sans-serif font is known to be easily readable on screens because of it's clean look. There is less user eye strain. Though the monospace is good for titles and headings it can come off with a 'stale' feel for longer texts. Thus why I chose a differnt font for the main text content fo the site
