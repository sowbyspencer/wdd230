Pleasant View Chamber of Commerce

The site name is simple and descriptive.

Site purpose

To have a place that people can easily find information about business and comunity ties. They will also be able to find and contact members of the chamber of commerce. Provide a way that people can contribute to recomendations for their comunity.

Logo

The logo shows a house, factory, and mountains. Pleasant View is a comunity with residential and comercial builings. It also is on the bench of a mountain. All parts of the image relate to the location and demographics of the comunity.
